The Aura Suite

Comprehensive Defense Against Academic Dishonesty

Biometric Identity

Aura initiates sessions only after strict facial verification using stored biometric data, ensuring the registered candidate is present throughout the duration.

Environment Lockdown

Our intelligent browser guard monitors tab switching and background activity, flagging any unauthorized navigation immediately.

Object Recognition

Real-time computer vision detects unauthorized items such as mobile phones, textbooks, or smart devices within the frame.

Attention Tracking

Advanced gaze estimation algorithms analyze eye movements to detect loss of focus or suspicious off-screen references.

Impersonation Control

Detects the presence of multiple faces or unauthorized individuals in the camera frame to prevent assistance during the exam.

Acoustic Analysis

Aura captures and analyzes ambient noise to identify whispers, speech patterns, or unauthorized communication.

Secure Infrastructure: All sensitive data is encrypted and securely stored in enterprise-grade PostgreSQL databases.
